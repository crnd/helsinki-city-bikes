Helsinki City Bikes

Helsinki City Bikes is a simple UI for quickly checking the status of Helsinki City Bike stations.

The bike stations are marked with green, yellow and red markers depending on the amount of bikes available at the station. By clicking/tapping on a bike station you will see a bit more detailed description of the station status.

The site automatically updates your location and the status of the bike stations so there is no reason to refresh the page.

The source code can be found from GitHub.
